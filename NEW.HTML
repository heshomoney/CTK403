What causes Stretch Marks: Stretch marks develop when the skin is being stretched beyond its normal capacity, as the skin stretches, the connective fibers that give it its suppleness may start to deteriorate and break beneath the surface of your skin. As a result, bands of discolouration appear on the skin's surface as a sort of scarring.

Over the years a lot of people have spent a fortune on tropical lotions, creams and other stretch mark remedies to remove stretch marks but the closest thing they were able to achieve was to make the scars less apparent and made the skin finer in texture. The only place where stretch marks are completely gotten rid of is in photo-manipulation softwares and with ai-plugin, therefore do not be duped if you are looking for a terrific stretch mark lotion that will hopefully get rid of them now. Even the before-and-after pictures merely make the scars less noticeable, leading you to believe that they have disappeared entirely when in reality, they are still present but hidden from view.

Stretch marks are influenced by genetics. Which can be either a positive or negative factor. If you have a family history of these beautiful lines (stretch marks), whether through pregnancy, puberty, or weight gain, you have a high likelihood of developing them yourself.

Now that the question of "removing stretch marks" has been resolved, let's move on to the real focus of article "How to truly lessen the visibility of your stretch mark.”

Stretch mark creams:

Chemical peels:

Cosmetic procedures:

Dermabrasion or an abdominoplasty (tummy tuck) may be advised in more extreme situations.
